RULES

Physics

  • Walls/Floors/Ceiling = obstacle.
  • Gravity: you fall down until a floor stops you.

Moves

  • RUN: move right. Wraps around to the left at the edge.
  • JUMP: move up-right, if no ceiling and no wall to the upper-right.

Chute

  • A chute switches the FRONT/BACK side of the card.
  • The player lands on the new side from the chute and gravity is applied.

Lever

  • A lever rotates the card 90° clockwise.

Note

  • Using chutes or levers is optional.
  • Remember to peek the other side of the card!

Goal

  • Stand on the GOAL cell.
